3 Introduction to Canvas

Canvas Student Tutorials (on YouTube) covers how to navigate Canvas, including the Dashboard and Global Navigation.

4 Additional Canvas Resources

Instructure (the Company that built Canvas) maintains Canvas Resources for Students which provides guidance on the various features of Canvas.

  • You may want to explore how to Update Your Profile. This page allows you to specify how your information is displayed in Canvas (e.g., name).

  • I recommend that you take a few minutes to watch Keep Track of Assignment Due Dates especially if you have not used Canvas’s Calendar functionality before.

  • Make sure that you know how to Check Your Grades. I recommend that you explore the What If functionality.
