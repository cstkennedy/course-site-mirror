1 What do you have to do?

Analyze the projects

  • Based upon contents of Functional Specs
  • Identifying Risks
  • Some will be known and some unknown

2 Risk Analysis

  • Basis for prototype design
  • Identify risks (tangible and intangible)
  • Prioritize risks based upon project impact & probability of occurance (scale of 1 - 5)
  • Identify methods for risk mitigation

3 Project Decisions & Risk

  • Uncertainties cannot be eliminated
  • Affects can be reduced or minimized

4 Some Mechanisms for Reduction

  • Establishing project action checklists
  • Team evaluation of planned actions
  • Critical evaluations by experts
  • Contingency plans for unexpected events

5 Mitigation Methods

  • Expert suggestions
  • Use of Best Practices
  • Change in processes/policies in customer domain
  • Customer cooperation/buy-in
  • All factors considered - including schedule and budget

6 What do you have to do?

  • Prepare documents that identify, assess, and manage the risks
  • Based upon expert/mentor advice and opinions

7 Remember…

  • Quality of Design is Critical
  • Establish a design approach that supports risk control
    • Sample inputs/outputs
    • Module interactions
    • Defined limitations of the system

8 Project Questions

  • Objective review - Have these changed?
  • What are the project factors?
  • What are the events critical to the overall project?
  • Is it still feasible?
