4 Module 4: Adding a Risk Matrix

The Module 4 Assignment is to create a risk matrix summarizing the analysis of technical and customer risks. The impact of each risk must be analyzed to determine - if the risk were to materialize, how critical would it impact the success of the project. This must be on a scale of one to five. Similarly, what is the likely hood of the risk occuring? Also, on a scale of one to five.

Prepare a Table with a scale of one to five on each “axis”. Probability on the x=axis and impact on the y-axis. A risk with impact and probability of 5 should be in the uppermost right corner of the matrix. Please visit www.cs.odu.edu/~cpi to see the matrices created by previous 410 teams.

Prioritize the risks as you label them. The most damaging and probable risk should be numbered as either T1 or C1, for technical and customer categories.

Also prepare a description of planned mitigations for each risk.

Make a section on your website for all of this documentation.
