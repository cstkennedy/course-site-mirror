Week 5 Deliverables

The lecture notes for this week list the questions to which you need answers. It is anticipated that you will find the needed information from the Internet, from your mentor, and from industry experts.

You are to publish the related information on your team’s website.
You should have received login information for your CS 410 account via email.

The following information must incrementally appear on your website by Sunday:

  1. Homepage
    1. Team color identified
    2. No team should have a project name yet, that is to be determined.
    3. Links to other pages.
  2. Team Biography Page
    1. Picture of each team member and mentor
    2. Bio for each team member and mentor
  3. Reference Page
    1. Link o all reference information for research performed
  4. Problem Description
    1. Identification of the generic societal problem (problem statement)
    2. Characteristics of the problem listed
    3. What is the current process used by the intended customer to deal with the problem today? (current process flow – should be a diagram)
    4. Identify where in the current process the problem occurs and where intervention is required.
