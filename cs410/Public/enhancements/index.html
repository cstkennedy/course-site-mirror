1 Skills Expected by an Employer

  • Justification presentations of a project
  • Evaluate alternatives for feasibility solutions Technical Specification Development
  • Project planning
  • Work Breakdown analysis
  • Cost estimation
  • Group Coordination and interaction
  • Interview skills for interaction with customer and domain experts
  • Cooperation skills
  • Present formal documents for project continuation
  • Produce specs suitable for production development

2 The Main Focus

  • Begin with a broad unstructured problem
  • Utilize (Introduce) program management skills required to achieve a structured solution to improve productivity of the process
  • Expose Costing Concepts including Direct Costs (labor, Equipment, etc.) and overhead considerations

3 Mission of 410

To consolidate key concepts of the undergraduate CS curriculum and to expose the students to “real world” applications of a variety of skills.
