1 Overview

This is a formal presentation of the Feasibility Slide Deck that your team has constructed over the past few weeks.

A microphone is required. A webcam is optional.

2 Participation

All team members must participate in the preparing the slide deck. However, your team has a fair bit of latitude in deciding who presents, what each person presents, and how slides are handed off from one speaker to the next.

While I would like for every member of every team to present… online courses exist because of the flexibility they can provide. If you are unable to attend the formal presentation…

  1. be sure to work with your team to prepare the materials

  2. email me (the teacher) to let me know.

3 Deliverables

Finalize the Feasibility Slide Deck and post it on your team’s website.
