Thomas J. Kennedy

Navigate to Discussions from the Navigation Menu and select the Personal Introduction forum. When constructing your post:

  1. At a minimum…

    • Provide your name (i.e., how you would like to be addressed).
    • State your degree program (major).

  2. Make a point of answering the following questions…

    • Why you are taking this course?
    • What do you expect to gain from it?

  3. (Optional) Consider discussing…

    • One or two personal pieces of information (e.g., Hobbies, Favourite Morris Story)
    • Why TNG (Picard) is better than TOS (Kirk).

  4. (Optional) Consider discussing…

    • Why Python is an excellent programming language.
    • Why Rust is an excellent programming language.
