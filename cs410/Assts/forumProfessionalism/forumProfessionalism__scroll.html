1 What is Professionalism?

This Professionalism Discussion has been part of the course since I began teaching it. Over the years… students have discussed many aspects of what it means to be a professional, e.g.,

  • Working “hard”
  • Work quality
  • Asking for help
  • Offering help
  • Self improvement & personal growth
  • Constructive feedback
  • Mutual respect
  • Open mind
  • Professional dress & hygiene (e.g., hair, perfume, cologne)
  • Mistakes
  • Communication (in-person)
  • Communication (written)
  • Communication (banter)
  • Respecting time
  • Setting work/life boundaries
  • Mentoring
  • Working with others
  • Collaboration
