Initial Group Evaluation

Briefly assess the performance of each of your team members – including yourself. For each team member, write a short paragraph (between 2-3 sentences).

 

Your team has been working together (in CS 411W) for just over three weeks. This is not long enough for a detailed (or complete) analysis. However, let us establish an initial baseline and identify potential issues.

After each paragraph specify the letter grade you think reflects each person’s work thus far. Any letter grades or evaluations found to be divorced from reality will be discarded.
