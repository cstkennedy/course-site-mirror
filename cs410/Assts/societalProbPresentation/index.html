1 Overview

This is the third (and final) iteration of your Individual Societal Problem. While this is titled as a “presentation”… it will actually be a written exercise.

2 Your Tasks

Your foundation for this exercise will be the combination of your Societal Problem Post and the follow-up discussion. You will draft a formal document that includes:

  1. A clear statement of your Societal Problem

  2. The background of your problem (e.g., characteristics and statistics)

  3. The Customers and End Users who will buy and use your software, respectively.

  4. A clear statement of your proposed solution.

  5. What your software will be (e.g., desktop application, web application, or mobile application).

  6. The tools and technologies that will be used for development and to build your software.

  7. A set of references in MLA or APA format.

Your document must take the form of a PDF. The standard grammar and composition rules apply.
