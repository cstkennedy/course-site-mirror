This is an evaluation of your contributions to the slide deck based on edit history within Google Slides. Everyone should both:

  • demonstrate the ability to generate and update content
  • update the slide deck with that content

This evaluation is not based purely on the number of contributions. It is based on:

  • quantity of contributions compared to the team as a whole
  • quality of contributions compared to the team as a whole
  • ongoing contributions (i.e. working continuously)
