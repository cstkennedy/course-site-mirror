1 Overview

You have already written brief evaluations of yourself and your teammates.

This exercise is intended as a self-reflection. Yes this type of exercise is somewhat cliché… I despised working through weekly self-reflection exercises as a student. However, the focus of this exercise is on your

  1. contributions to the team project with a focus on quality.
  2. contributions to the team project with a focus on quantity.
  3. communication with your team.
  4. presentation skills.

2 What to Submit

Take a few minutes to consider your overall contribution to the project. Answer the following questions:

  1. What would you describe as your main (most important) contribution thus far?

  2. Are there any areas where you would like to increase your contribution?

  3. How would you rate your communication and collaboration thus far?

  4. How have your presentation skills improved since the beginning of the semester?

  5. If you had to choose a skill to improve (large or small)… what skill and how?

When grading your evaluation… I will focus on:

  1. Completeness of your answers
  2. Overall grammar and style (in the context of clarity)
  3. Accuracy and conciseness. Do not pad your answers.

A reasonable length is around 75% of a page.

Submit a PDF of your work in Canvas.
